#### Release Highlights

Aspose.BarCode for C++ 24.11 for Linux enhances decoding quality for 1D barcodes with new QualitySettings support and corrects a critical bug related to MaxiCode codetext construction.

#### New Features and Fixes

- **1D Barcode QualitySettings**:
  - Tuned scanning sensitivity and accuracy under suboptimal image conditions.
  - Improved performance and adaptability in diverse environments.

- **MaxiCode Exception Fix**:
  - Corrected potential `NullPointerException` from `GetConstructedCodetext()` method.

#### Public API

- No breaking or structural changes introduced in the API.
