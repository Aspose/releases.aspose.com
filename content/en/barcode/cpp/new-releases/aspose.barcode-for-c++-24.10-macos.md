#### Release Highlights

Aspose.BarCode for C++ 24.10 for macOS includes critical updates to the barcode detection engine, specifically targeting hangs during recognition and boosting QR decoding reliability. It ensures robust scanning across all major 2D barcode formats.

#### Enhancement & Fixes

- **Enhanced QR Code Recognition**:
  - Refined detection algorithms to reduce processing delay and improve accuracy on all platforms.

- **Fix for Barcode Engine Hangs**:
  - The barcode engine no longer hangs while processing the following symbologies:
    - Aztec, DataMatrix, DotCode, HanXin, QR, Pdf417, MicroPdf417
