### TrueType Style Attributes Support

Take your C# font manipulation applications to the next level with the ability to leverage the TrueType Style Attributes table. This feature addition enables developers to perform in-depth font analysis and manipulation within their solutions.

### New Classes and Properties

Aspose.Font for .NET 24.6 lets you explore the new `TtfStatTable` class and its properties to access intricate details within the Style Attributes table.
