# Export Hungarian Special Characters

Previously in some instances the Hungarian special characters were wrongly exported from DWG to PDF. In this release of the API, the aforementioned issue has been resolve.

# Improved DWG TO PDF conversion

Fixed the `OutOfMemoryError` while converting `DWG` TO `PDF` format.

# Improved Scene Moving Sensitivity

Improved the sensitivity of the moving scene.
