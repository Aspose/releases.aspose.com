#### Release Highlights

Aspose.CAD for .NET 25.1 (MSI) delivers a robust set of enhancements including **support for PostScript**, blind watermarking across formats (GLTF/GLB), optimized SVG layer reading, and improved DWG, OBJ, STP, and DGN handling. It also refines vectorization with solid bold lines and adds support for Docker-specific rendering contexts.

- **SVG Layer Extraction**  
  Layer-level information is now extracted when reading SVG files. [CADNET-9933]

- **Blind Watermarking Implementation**  
  Comprehensive watermark embedding, extraction, and removal across GLTF/GLB, DWG, and PDF. [CADNET-9406, CADNET-9808, CADNET-9624]

- **Vertex Grouping via Douglas-Peucker Algorithm**  
  Adds geometric simplification for vectorization tasks. [CADNET-9404]
