#### Release Highlights

Aspose.CAD for .NET 25.3 (MSI) delivers substantial upgrades for CAD professionals by adding **support for Navisworks NWF files**, improving **Hatch and LWPolyline writing for DXF 2018**, and expanding capabilities in **3D scale transformations** across FBX, DXF, and OBJ formats. The update also boosts rendering accuracy and stability during complex DWG-to-DXF conversions and STP surface operations.
