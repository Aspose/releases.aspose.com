# Enhanced CPIO Archive Compression Compatibility

Support to compress `CPIO` archives to `GZIP`, `BZIP2`, & `XZ` archive compression. While the `TAR` and `CPIO` formats are also respected while saving to `BZIP2`.

# Support for Snappy Algorithm

Added the support for the snappy algorithm that offers high-speed and good compression.
