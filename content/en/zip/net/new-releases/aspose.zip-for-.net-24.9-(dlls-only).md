#### Release Highlights

The DLLs-only release of Aspose.ZIP for .NET 24.9 focuses on event-driven architecture and performance visibility. It introduces progress tracking, event hooks, and deferred entry creation across a wide range of archive formats.

### ‚ú≥Ô∏è New Feature

- **Archive Event Hooks for XAR & ISO**:
  - Added new types including `EventsBag`, `EntryEventArgs`, and `CancelEntryEventArgs` to monitor and optionally cancel compression actions.
  - Enables advanced customization and diagnostics.

- **Compression & Extraction Progress APIs**:
  - Progress event handlers now available for:
    - `LzmaArchiveSettings.CompressionProgressed`
    - `ZArchiveLoadOptions.ExtractionProgressed`
    - `ZstandardSaveOptions.CompressionProgressed`
    - `Bzip2LoadOptions.ExtractionProgressed`, and more.
  - Ideal for UI integrations or monitoring tools.

- **Lazy Stream Entry Creation**:
  - New `CreateEntry()` method with `Func<Stream>` support improves performance when handling large or on-demand entry data.

### üîß Enhancement

- **Metered License Expansion**:
  - You can now build self-extracting archives while operating under a metered license, aligning this capability with standard license behavior.
