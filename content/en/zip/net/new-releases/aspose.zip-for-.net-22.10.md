# Get 7z Entry Modification Time

Ability to Get the last (most recent) modified date and time for 7z entry. Display modification time for 7z entry.

Ability to extract the 7z (7 Zip) entry to the provided stream.

Ability to Get or Set encoding on zip archive extraction.
