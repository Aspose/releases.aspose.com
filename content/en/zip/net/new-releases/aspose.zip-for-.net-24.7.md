This release of Aspose.ZIP for .NET 24.7 (MSI installer) delivers valuable enhancements and bug fixes to better organize your archive management workflows within your C# and VB.NET applications.

### Enriched 7z Security

The `EncryptHeader` property in `SevenZipEncryptionSettings` in the latest .NET ZIP archives API offers advanced 7z archive headers encryption and adds an extra layer of protection.

### Improved ISO Handling

Aspose.ZIP for .NET now offers more robust handling of ISO archives to ascertain seamless integration with your workflows.

### Multiple Archive Extraction Fixes

We have added fixes for several bugs affecting the extraction of Zstandard, Gzip, Z, and Bzip2 archives to guarantee reliable archive processing within your C# applications.


> You can view the list of all new features, enhancements, and bug fixes introduced in this release by visiting [Aspose.ZIP for .NET 24.7 Release Notes](https://releases.aspose.com/zip/net/release-notes/2024/aspose-zip-for-net-24-7-release-notes/).
