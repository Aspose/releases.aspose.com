Aspose.Slides for .NET 25.2 delivers precise audio control features, quality improvements in shape thumbnails, and fixes across SVG, ODP, PPTM, and chart rendering during format conversions.

- **Ligature Control**: Disable font ligatures during HTML, SVG, and other exports.
