Aspose.PSD for .NET 25.4 introduces support for inline images in AI content streams, resolves key rendering issues in Illustrator shading, and adds artifacts targeting .NET 8 and .NET 9 platforms.

- **AI Shading (Type 2) Rendering**: Corrected multiple issues affecting shading types in `.ai` documents, enhancing gradient and path visual fidelity.
- **Bottom Crop Bug**: Fixed an issue where the **bottom part of AI images was cropped** during rendering.
- **Framework-Specific Bug Fixes**:
