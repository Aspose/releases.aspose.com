#### Release Highlights

Aspose.HTML for .NET 24.9 delivers performance boosts, better rendering accuracy, and improved image conversion workflows. This release focuses on enhancing PDF and PNG conversion, nested layout rendering, and constructor optimization, while laying the groundwork to remove dependencies on System.Drawing.Common in upcoming versions.
