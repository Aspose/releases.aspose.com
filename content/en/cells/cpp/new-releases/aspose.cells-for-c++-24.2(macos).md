Mac developers, unlock the full potential of your spreadsheets with Aspose.Cells for C++ 24.2! This macOS-friendly update brings many new features to enhance your app's functionality:

### Key Highlights

- **Unleash Spreadsheet Magic with UUID API:** Manage unique identifiers effortlessly, ensuring seamless integration across platforms.
- **Explore Data with Ease:** Leverage the enhanced "convert to number" menu for cells storing numbers as text, and streamline data analysis on your Mac.
- **Native macOS Performance:** Experience optimized performance tailored specifically for macOS environments.
- **Seamless Integration:** Integrate seamlessly with your existing macOS development tools and workflows.
- **Bug Fixes and Enhancements:** Enjoy a suite of bug fixes and improvements for a smoother and more reliable development experience on macOS.
