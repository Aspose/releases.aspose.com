#### Release Highlights

Aspose.Cells for .NET 24.10 brings high-impact updates for Excel developers. This release includes support for compact PivotTables, improved formula control, better handling of blank rows and columns, and extended GridWeb and GridJs functionality. Ideal for advanced reporting, conversion, and spreadsheet automation workflows.
