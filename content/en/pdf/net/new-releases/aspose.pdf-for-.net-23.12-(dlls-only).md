Are you looking to efficiently handle PDFs in your .NET apps? If yes, try Aspose.PDF for .NET 23.12 version, which streamlines PDF processing by reducing development time and resources. Build robust PDF solutions with features like optimized conversion, forms management, and more.

### PDF to Markdown Conversion

Effortlessly convert your PDFs to Markdown format, preserving text formatting and structure. Converting PDF to MD could be perfect for knowledge base creation or content sharing.

### Retrieve Text by Subtype/Form

With this release of the C# API, you can extract text based on specific subtypes or form fields within your PDF documents, which provides greater control over text retrieval.

### Introduced OFD to PDF Conversion 

This release introduces OFD to PDF conversion, allowing you to seamlessly convert your OFD files to PDF documents in your C# apps and expanding your document conversion options.

### Improved Font Handling

The enhancements to font handling in this release ensure accurate rendering of mixed Arabic and English text within your PDFs.

### TIFF Conversion 

Convert PDFs to TIFF images with a choice of color depth and unlock more possibilities, covering various use cases.

### Bug Fixes

This release addresses a multitude of reported issues, providing a more stable and reliable PDF processing experience.
