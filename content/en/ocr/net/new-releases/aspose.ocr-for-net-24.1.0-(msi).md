Simplify your .NET development process with improvements for searchable PDFs in Aspose.OCR for .NET 24.1.0 (MSI installer). Experience accurate text overlay positioning and fixed rendering issues.

### Sharper Searchable PDFs

Integrate optimized searchable PDFs into your optical character recognition (OCR) workflows with the latest .NET OCR API release. It offers improved text overlay positioning and enhances your exported PDFs' searchability.

### Accurate Dark Area Rendering

We have fixed the issues related to the dark areas in background images getting rendered with inverted colors in version 24.1.0 of Aspose.OCR for .NET.
