We are excited to announce the availability of Aspose.Finance for .NET 24.8 (MSI) release. This release empowers .NET developers with an XBRL validation boost and features a convenient installation experience through the MSI installer.

### XBRL Validation Upgrades

This release of the .NET financial reporting API strengthens XBRL validation to guarantee the accuracy and integrity of your financial data.

### Effortless Installation

By leveraging the MSI installer, you can experience straightforward and efficient integration of Aspose.Finance into your .NET projects.
