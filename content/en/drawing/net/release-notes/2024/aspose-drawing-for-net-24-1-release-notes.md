## **Major Features**
Several improvements and features were added this month. The most notable are:

- Improved correctness of vector regions and enabled them by default (DRAWINGNET-1452)
- Implemented support for subpixel rectangles in clipping and regions (DRAWINGNET-963 and DRAWINGNET-1196)
- Fixed Region.GetBounds() for large regions (DRAWINGNET-1203)
- Added Aspose.Drawing.Common DLLs for direct download (DRAWINGNET-1461)
